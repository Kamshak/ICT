About Us

Coffees

Our coffee comes from hand-picked suppliers who live up to highest standards in quality, sustainability and fair trade. These select coffee beans are roasted to perfection in house. As we obtain our coffee beans in small batches to serve you fresh coffee every time, our selection of coffees will vary weekly. Do not be afraid to try a new roast and let our baristas help you find the beans that best match your taste.

Cold Pressed Juices

Our delicous, cold pressed juices are supplied by Green Beards in Ranelagh. They use seasonal fruit by local producers which are cold pressed to keep all vitamins and deliciousness from the fresh fruit inside of the juice. For our seasonally changing selection of juices please ask your barista.

Breakfast Slow down you move too fast - got to make the morning last!

The way you start your day carries sets pace for the entire day. Get it right by starting it with some of our amazingly delicous and healthy breakfast dishes. Try our famous scones or eggs benedict!

Pastries
